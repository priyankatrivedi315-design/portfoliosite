About Me

I am Sudha Murthy

A storyteller, educator
& philanthropist
from India

I have dedicated my life to literature, education, and social change. As a bestselling author of over 30 books, a Padma Bhushan awardee, and the chairperson of the Infosys Foundation, I believe that simple acts of compassion can transform lives. I invite you to explore the stories and ideas that have shaped my journey.
