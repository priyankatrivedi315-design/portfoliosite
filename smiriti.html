ABOUT ME

I'm Smriti Irani, a proud Indian who has worn many hats — from a beloved television actress who played Tulsi Virani in Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi, to a dynamic politician and former Union Minister.

Born in New Delhi, I have dedicated my life to public service, women empowerment, and strengthening India's cultural fabric. Passionate about education, textiles, and creating meaningful impact.
